What is this?

VITB Cafés is your one-stop guide to all 5 campus canteens at VIT Bhopal. Browse the complete menu, discover the best-rated dishes, and help fellow VITians eat better by sharing your reviews.

How to use
1
Pick a café from the tab bar — Mayuri, Underbelly, Spl Block, Dakshin, or Bistro.
2
Filter by category (Dosa, Pizza, Momos…) to browse faster. Items are shown best-rated first.
3
Search any dish across all cafés at once using the search bar — results are ranked by rating.
4
Rate & review any dish by clicking the button on its card. Your rating helps the community!
5
Send feedback via the Feedback section below — suggest new dishes, report pricing issues, or request features.
